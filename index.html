About

Hi! I am Nyleko, a Montreal and London (UK) based artist. Much of my work is characterized by pop art style portraits. In addition to my pop art pieces, I also enjoy working in acrylics, watercolours, and have a hobby in hand lettering.

Please feel free to browse my art prints which I will be putting up for sale very soon!
